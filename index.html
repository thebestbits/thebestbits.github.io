PRIVACY POLICY FOR KIDS TALK

This privacy policy governs your use of the software application Kids Talk for mobile devices that was created by The Best Bits LLC. The Application allows for children to make actual phone calls.

What information does the application obtain and how is it used?

  • User Provided Information: When you add a contact and use the application, you generally provide access to your phone contacts a) saved name or nickname, b) phone number, and c) contact photo. The application will access user's photos and camera roll only after permission is granted within the application.

  • Automatically Collected Information: The application does not automatically collect any information. This Application does not collect precise information about the location of your mobile device.

  • Third-parties: There are no third parties that see and/or have access to information obtained by the application.

What are my opt-out rights?

You can stop all collection of information by the Application easily by uninstalling the Application. You may use the standard uninstall processes as may be available as part of your mobile device.

Children

We do not use the Application to knowingly solicit data from or market to children under the age of 13. If a parent or guardian becomes aware that his or her child has provided us with information without their consent, he or she should contact us at derr@thebestbits.co. We will delete such information from our files within a reasonable time.
